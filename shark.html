White Shark

Scientists say people may pose more of a threat to great whites than the sharks pose to people.

  • Scientific Name: Carcharodon carcharias
  • Average Length: Female: 15 – 21 ft., Male: 11 – 13 ft.
  • Mass: 1,500 – 2,400 lbs (Adult)
  • Speed: 25 mph (Maximum, In Short Bursts)

    • The great white shark, commonly known as the great white or the white shark, is a species of large mackerel shark which can be found in the coastal surface waters of all the major oceans.
